Public Grievance

Services

How to Complain

You can complain about problems with government services through websites, phone numbers, or by writing a letter.

What Happens Next

Your complaint gets a special number, and people check if it's a real problem. They then try to fix it.

Quick Solutions

They want to fix problems fast, so they set a deadline to solve each complaint. If it's not fixed, there are other people who can help.

Keep Things Private

Your complaint is private. They don't tell everyone about it. They keep it safe.

Tell Everyone What They Do

Every year, they share a report that tells everyone how many complaints they got and how many they fixed.

Ask for More Help

If you're not happy with how they fixed your problem, you can ask other people to check if it's fair.
